### The Global LGBTQ+ Travel, Safety, & Health Ecosystem

Gay World News is an all-encompassing digital hub designed for the LGBTQ+ or gay community. 
Combining high-level safety data with localized travel guides and videos and essential sexual health resources, legal status and security, it ensures users can navigate the world with awareness.

## âœ¨ Core Features
- **Comprehensive Travel Guides**: Curated recommendations for LGBTQ+-friendly hotels, the best nightlife, and "must-visit" landmarks, supported by immersive video content and local people.
- **Safety & Legal Intelligence**: Real-time tracking of global discrimination levels, legal status, and safety ratings for every country.
- **Sexual Health Hub**: Localized health support and U=U (Undetectable = Untransmittable) advocacy, connecting users to nearby clinics and resources whether they are local or visiting.
- **Fixed-Map Navigation**: A responsive, centered world map optimized for seamless browsing on PCs, tablets, and smartphones.
- **Emergency UI**: High-visibility "Red Navigation" Exit button designed for quick, discreet exits to their own YouTube account.
