Our Mission

We love to travel and with like-minded individuals we have created this website to keep ourselves and the LGBTQ+ community safe in a volatile and changing world. Whether you are looking for assistance in a country you live in or are traveling or relocating, we do our best to provide up-to-date advice on not only the nightlife and places to visit and stay, but also most importantly, the security, legal status, and healthcare accessibility - providing a specialized focus on HIV medical services and other LGBTQ+ care.

When planning a trip or arriving somewhere with no plan, we have been amazed at the lack of information available in the modern day. If a country is somewhat developed, the information tends to be about the gay night life, some suggested hotels and not much else. If it is a developing country or not even started, then you are unlikely to find any meaningful information at all. That is our challenge: A mission to cut out the fluff and focus on the facts. With 197 countries and territories, it is impossible to cover each one ourselves, so we intend to invite LGBTQ+ people all over the world who would like to see their work published, be it in video form or text, to help us with on-the-ground information.

Until then, we will share our experience while also using tough AI filters to strip away the sensationalism and gossip and bypass that noise to bring you the hidden, vital news that allows you to make safe decisions and have the best possible time.
